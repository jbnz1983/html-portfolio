The Best Movies According to Jamie

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Steel Magnolias

A heartwarming story about the trials and tribulations of life amongst a group of women that are friends.

Step Mom

A story of a young brother and sister who have trouble adapating to their new Step Mom all whilst their Mother is facing a cancer diagnosis.

The Devil Wears Prada

A new university journalism graduate finds it difficult working in the fashion industry as an assistant to one of the industry's toughest veterans.
